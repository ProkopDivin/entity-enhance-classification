Lanškroun

Lanškroun (; ) is a town in Ústí nad Orlicí District in the Pardubice Region of the Czech Republic. It has about 9,800 inhabitants. The town is located in the Orlické Foothills, on the border of the historical lands of Bohemia and Moravia.
Lanškroun is an industrial town with representation of the electronics, engineering and paper industries. The historic town centre is well preserved and is protected as an urban monument zone. The main landmark of Lanškroun is the Renaissance town hall.
